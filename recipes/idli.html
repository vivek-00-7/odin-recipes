idli

Wanna know about it?

Description
Idli or idly is a type of savoury rice cake, originating from the Indian subcontinent, popular as breakfast foods in Southern India and in Sri Lanka. The cakes are made by steaming a batter consisting of fermented black lentils (de-husked) and rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cup basmati rice
  • 1/2 tablespoon fenugreek seeds
  • 5 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 11/2 cup urad dal
  • salt as required
  • water as required

Steps

  1. Soak the rice & dal and grid into a past & combine
  2. Let the batter ferment, then add salt.
  3. Transfer the idli batter in the idli stand
  4. Wait for the utensil to cool a bit before scooping the lidlis out
